Welcome to the Home of the Beastie Bot.

The Beastie Bot is an auto-rap generator that takes a theme and spits out a hot Beastie Boys inspired rap. This project was created for a Software Design class at Olin College by Mackenzie Frackleton (@frackleton), Bill Wong (@billmwong), Emily Mamula (@emamula), and Robert Siegel (@robertsiegel).
